SUPPLIERS

Infra Tech is committed to developing robust supply chains that support our system integration philosophy. Suppliers are an integral part of our supply chain strategy, and we continually seek high-performing suppliers to strengthen our operations.

Our Supplier Selection is Based on:

  • Quality Performance: High-quality products and services.
  • Leadership: Strong leadership and vision.
  • Financial Stability: Ensured long-term viability.
  • Continuous Improvement: Commitment to ongoing enhancements.
  • Cost Competitiveness: Competitive pricing.
  • Early Involvement: Active in product development.
  • EH&S Compliance: Strict adherence to environmental, health, and safety standards.

To be a Preferred Supplier, You Should:

  • Implement Lean Manufacturing: Efficient production practices.
  • Maintain Cost Competitiveness: Focus on cost-efficiency.
  • Commit to Regressive Pricing: Year-over-year cost reductions.
  • Understand Low-Cost Sourcing: Value-driven sourcing strategies.
  • Offer Flexible Terms: Adaptable financial arrangements.
  • Ensure Short Cycle Times: Efficient production and delivery.
  • Provide Clear Metrics: Measurable performance indicators.
  • Deliver On-Time, Defect-Free: Consistent quality and reliability.
  • Meet Aerospace Quality Standards: Relevant quality certifications.
  • Commit to EH&S: Strong environmental, health, and safety practices.
  • Solve Problems Proactively: Independent issue resolution.

Infra Tech values partners who share our commitment to excellence and continuous improvement.
