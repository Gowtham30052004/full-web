ABOUT US

Infra Tech is a vertically integrated web design and embedded systems solutions provider catering to the technology-driven products industry. Infra Tech's key strength is founded on over two decades of experience in developing high-reliability products in this domain with the broad capability to develop and manufacture any high-reliability product. Infra Tech has developed several electronic systems for its customers with the support of its captive manufacturing facility and attention to detail. Infra Tech achieves its philosophy of manufacturing products with the reliability and lifespan expected in its market domain.

Infra Tech is recognized by its customers for its ability to deliver complex programs in short turnaround times with ease.

Histroy of Infra Tech

Infra Tech was founded by two innovative minds, Gowtham and Kavin, during the final months of their third year in college. Driven by a shared passion for technology and design, they conceived the idea for Infra Tech during a late-night brainstorming session. Their vision was to create a company that could deliver high-reliability web design and embedded systems solutions.

Future

Early Years

Upon graduating, Gowtham and Kavin officially established Infra Tech, focusing initially on providing bespoke web design services. Their commitment to quality and customer-centric approach quickly earned them a reputation for excellence. This early success laid a strong foundation for the company's growth.

Innovation and Development

Innovation is at the core of Infra Tech's philosophy. The company continuously invests in research and development to stay ahead of technological advancements and market trends. Infra Tech's R&D team, composed of industry experts, works diligently to create cutting-edge solutions that meet and exceed customer expectations.

Future Outlook

Looking to the future, Infra Tech remains dedicated to its core values of innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction. The company continues to explore new markets and expand its product offerings, aiming to remain at the forefront of the web design and embedded systems industry.
