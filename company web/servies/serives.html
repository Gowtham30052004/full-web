Service Life Cycle and Timelines

Infra Tech supports customers throughout their product development life cycle with a structured approach, deploying services in five phases: Transition, Initial Operational (Pilot), Steady, Continuous Improvement, and Reversibility.

Transition Phase

In the Transition Phase, we conduct a comprehensive initial assessment and develop a detailed transition plan. This involves deploying a skilled team and initiating developmental activities. We thoroughly study various design aspects, applications, platforms, test setups, and equipment. Knowledge acquisition is key, as we collect all existing documentation and create new ones where necessary. This phase ensures a solid foundation for all subsequent activities, minimizing risks and setting clear objectives.

Initial Operational Phase

During the Initial Operational Phase, Infra Tech begins servicing developmental requests from clients. We integrate client requirements into our developmental activities, ensuring alignment with their expectations. This phase involves capturing and analyzing service data to establish benchmarks and service level goals. By closely monitoring performance, we can make necessary adjustments to meet the client's needs. This phase sets the stage for a smooth transition into steady operations, with a focus on quality and efficiency.

Steady Phase

In the Steady Phase, Infra Tech provides developmental services according to the mutually agreed program plan. The workflow becomes fully settled, and we focus on maintaining high service levels. Continuous improvements are made to processes and the knowledge base, ensuring optimal performance. Regular assessments help us identify areas for enhancement and implement best practices. This phase is characterized by stability, reliability, and a commitment to meeting client goals.

Reversibility Phase

In the Reversibility Phase, Infra Tech ensures a seamless transfer of knowledge and assets back to the client. We systematically document and hand over all necessary information, ensuring continuity of operations. This phase includes thorough training and support to enable the client's team to take over seamlessly. By maintaining clear communication and providing ongoing assistance, we ensure a smooth transition. This phase underscores our commitment to client satisfaction and long-term partnerships.

Infra Tech is dedicated to supporting your product development needs with efficiency, reliability, and a commitment to continuous improvement.
